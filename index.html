TransparencyAssumptions

What we assume – and what we measure.

We assume:
  • Observational data contains no hidden confounders (causal estimates).
  • Stationary failure rate within a 500‑observation window (recalibration).
  • Independent uncertainty sources (conservative over‑escalation).
We measure & gate:
  • Propensity AUC ≥ 0.6 – otherwise causal adjustment disabled.
  • ECE > 0.1 triggers prior update.
  • Memory weight bounded to 0.2 – Bayesian prior remains dominant.
Epistemic humility builds more trust than over‑claiming certainty.
