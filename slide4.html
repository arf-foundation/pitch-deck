The Solution: ARF Core Engine

Hybrid Bayesian‑Deterministic Control Plane
Bayesian Risk Engine
Computes P(risk|signals) using hierarchical priors and Hamiltonian Monte Carlo (HMC). Calibrated uncertainty intervals.
Semantic Memory
FAISS‑based vector store retrieves similar past incidents to calibrate risk priors in real time.
Deterministic Policy Thresholds (DPT)
Convert probabilistic scores into binary actions with full auditability.
if P(risk) < 0.2 → APPROVE
elif P(risk) > 0.8 → DENY & HEAL
else → ESCALATE (Human‑in‑the‑loop)
Healing Orchestrator
Executes pre‑approved recovery plans (rollback, quarantine) without human intervention when risk is high.
