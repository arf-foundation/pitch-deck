Current Limitations: Observability ≠ Governance

Why dashboards fail to control autonomous agents

CONVENTIONAL OBSERVABILITY

Reactive & Partial
Relies on logs/traces after failure. Blind to prompt drift and agent internal state.
Human Bottlenecks
Operators cannot interpret agent traces at machine speed; alert fatigue leads to missed signals.
Emergent Blind Spots
Misses latent coupling in multi‑agent systems and non‑stationary behavior.

ARF GOVERNANCE

Bayesian Risk Modeling
Quantifies uncertainty before execution (pre‑emptive).
Deterministic Policy
Enforces auditable thresholds (Approve/Deny/Escalate) with full traceability.
Autonomous Self-Healing
Automatically triggers rollback, throttle, or quarantine actions.
Outcome: Shift from passive monitoring to bounded, deterministic control.
